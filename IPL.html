IPL: Behind Closed Doors

Committee Description

The Indian Premier League (IPL) Committee is one of the most exciting and entertaining simulations that SVCEMUNSOC offers. While many Model United Nations committees focus on serious issues of diplomacy, policy, and governance, the IPL brings a refreshing twist by combining the thrill of sports with the spirit of debate.

In this committee, delegates step into the shoes of franchise owners, team managers, players, sponsors, or members of the cricket board. They negotiate trades, build strategies, debate controversies, and make crucial decisions that can change the fate of their teams. From handling sponsorship deals to bidding wars in auctions, every session is packed with energy, competition, and fun.

The IPL committee proves that MUN is not only about serious discussions on global politics but also about creating spaces where participants can relax, laugh, and bond through their love of sports. It adds a lighter, more playful dimension to the overall conference experience.

Through this, SVCEMUNSOC shows its commitment to balancing serious diplomacy with enjoyable experiences, ensuring that every delegate leaves with knowledge, memories, and smiles.
