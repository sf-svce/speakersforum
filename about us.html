Our Vision

The Students’ Forum envisions becoming a dynamic platform that transforms voices into influence, ideas into impact, and individuals into leaders. We seek to build a culture where every student— regardless of background—finds the courage to speak, the clarity to inspire, and the stage to shine. Our mission is to nurture confidence, creativity, and leadership, turning students into changemakers who uplift one another and leave a lasting impact.

What We Do

MENTORSHIP: Language training beyond college hours is given exclusively for all the students who volunteer for this program through a detailed syllabus provided by a top corporate company. In addition, students are taught public speaking by doing ice-breaking sessions and workshops.

LANGUAGE TRAINING: Continuous exclusive sessions are held to enhance students’ soft skills like people skills, leadership, etc., to get through interviews and also to communicate effectively in their future academic as well as professional settings.

WORKSHOPS: Geopolitics, Indian Polity, and performative speaking arts are taught to interested students in respective societies (MUN, DEBATE, and CULTURAL). Students also represent the college in external events through the MUN Delegation, Debate Contingent, and Cultural team.

PRESENTATIONS: We regularly train students to present their papers, pitch ideas during hackathons, and deliver effective presentations. We emphasize verbal and non-verbal etiquette and give students the practice to confidently answer questions at these events.

How We Do

At Speakers’ Forum, we know what it feels like to have something to say, but not know how to say it. We’ve all been there, hands shaking, voice trembling, thoughts racing. That’s why we’ve built a space where your voice is acknowledged and made stronger. ,

Here, we don’t throw you on a stage and say, “go speak.”. We walk with you, step by step, building the confidence to stand tall, the clarity to speak well, and the presence to be remembered.

Here’s how we make it happen:

• We recreate real pressure through MUNs, debates, and cultural events, not to scare, but to prepare you for the world. You’ll learn how to stay calm under pressure, speak with purpose, and think on your feet like the leaders you admire as you step into their shoes and discuss pressing issues.

• Our public speaking classes are spaces for honest feedback, personal growth, and supportive learning.

• Through sessions on resume writing, group discussions, and interview skills, we help you grow into someone who not only sounds confident but feels confident in any room.

• With Computer-Assisted Language Learning, you’ll get to fine-tune your accent, pronunciation, and fluency.

In a world full of speakers, be the one they remember.
