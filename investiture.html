The New Regime

The Office Bearers for the academic year 2025–2026 have taken charge with energy, vision, and a strong commitment to elevate the Speakers’ Forum to new heights. Representing the diverse talents of SVCE, this team brings together leadership, creativity, and a passion for nurturing articulate, confident voices. Their primary focus is to build upon the Forum’s existing legacy while introducing fresh initiatives that broaden engagement across the student community. They aim to provide every member a platform to express ideas freely, participate in debates, and develop critical thinking skills. Beyond organizing events, the 2025–2026 Office Bearers are committed to fostering a culture of inclusivity, collaboration, and professional excellence. They envision a year of impactful workshops, challenging simulations, and signature events that inspire dialogue and encourage participation at all levels. With clear objectives, strategic planning, and unwavering dedication, this team is poised to strengthen the Forum’s presence on campus, promote meaningful discourse, and empower members to become confident communicators, leaders, and socially aware citizens ready to take on responsibilities within and beyond SVCE.

Legacy Of The Forum

The Office Bearers of 2024–2025 completed their tenure leaving behind a legacy of dedication, creativity, and impactful initiatives. Throughout the year, they expanded the reach of the Speakers’ Forum, organizing a variety of events that encouraged students to develop public speaking, critical thinking, and leadership skills. Their approach combined structured planning with innovative ideas, ensuring that every workshop, debate, and simulation was both engaging and educational. Emphasis on mentorship and guidance allowed members to step out of their comfort zones, express themselves confidently, and actively participate in Forum activities. By blending established practices with new strategies, the 2024–2025 Office Bearers strengthened the organization’s framework, enhanced its reputation, and set high standards for professionalism. Their focus on collaboration, accountability, and inclusivity fostered a supportive environment where all members felt valued and motivated. The team’s achievements and strategic foresight have created a solid foundation for the incoming 2025–2026 Office Bearers to build upon, inspiring them to aim higher, innovate further, and carry forward the Forum’s vision with renewed energy.

Forging New Paths

The newly inducted Office Bearers of 2025–2026 step into their roles with a clear vision, dedication, and a drive to elevate the Speakers’ Forum to unprecedented heights. We extend our heartfelt gratitude to the previous team for their guidance, commitment, and leadership, which have created a strong foundation for our journey ahead. Inspired by their accomplishments, we aim to push boundaries, introduce innovative initiatives, and set ambitious goals for the Forum this year. Our focus will be on expanding flagship events, hosting unique workshops, enhancing member engagement, and creating opportunities that challenge, inspire, and empower students. By fostering collaboration, creativity, and leadership, we aspire to cultivate an environment where members can express ideas confidently, think critically, and participate meaningfully. With determination, teamwork, and strategic planning, the 2025–2026 Office Bearers pledge to uphold the Forum’s values while setting new benchmarks of excellence, leaving a lasting impact on our community and inspiring future generations of speakers and leaders.
